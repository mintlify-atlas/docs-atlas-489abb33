## Registering an account

New users register at `/register`. All accounts are created with the `Viatger` role by default.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the registration page">
    Navigate to `/register`. The page is publicly accessible — no login required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Complete all required fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | **Nombre Completo** (`full_name`) | Your full name, e.g. *Juan Pérez* |
    | **Correo electrónico** (`email`) | A valid email address used to log in |
    | **Contraseña** (`password`) | Minimum 6 characters |

    <Note>
      The `rol` field is set to `Viatger` automatically. You cannot select a different role at registration.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Registrarse**. The frontend sends `POST /auth/register` with your details. On success, you are redirected to `/login`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Logging in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the login page">
    Navigate to `/login`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Enter the **Correo electrónico** and **Contraseña** you registered with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Entrar">
    The frontend sends `POST /auth/login` (as `application/x-www-form-urlencoded` with `username` and `password` fields, as required by OAuth2). On success, a JWT access token is stored in the session and you are redirected to `/dashboard`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If you see "Email o contraseña incorrectos", double-check that you are using the email address you registered with. Passwords are case-sensitive.
</Warning>

## Browsing the trip list

After logging in you land on `/dashboard`, the main trip discovery page.

- The page calls `GET /viatge/trips` and displays all available trips as cards.
- Each card shows the trip name, destination (`desti`), dates, and current status (`estat`).
- If no trips exist yet, the page shows an informational message.

<Tip>
  Trips with status `Actiu` are currently underway. Look for trips with status `Planificant` if you want to enroll before departure.
</Tip>

## Viewing trip details

Click any trip card on the dashboard to open its detail page at `/trips/:id`.

The detail page calls `GET /viatge/trips/{id}` and displays:

| Section | Information shown |
|---------|------------------|
| Header | Trip name (`nom`), destination badge, date range, status badge |
| Description | Full trip description (`descripcio`) and cover image if provided |
| Info list | Destination, start date (`data_inici`), end date (`data_fi`), participant count vs. capacity (`maxim_participants`) |
| Actions panel | Enroll button (if not yet enrolled), or "Inscrito" confirmation |

The page has two tabs: **Información** (always visible) and **Chat del Grupo** (visible only to enrolled users and Admins).

## Enrolling in a trip

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the trip detail page">
    Navigate to `/trips/:id` for the trip you want to join.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Inscribirse ahora">
    The **Inscribirse ahora** button appears in the actions panel on the right side of the page. It is only shown when your role is `Viatger` and you are not yet enrolled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The frontend sends `POST /viatge/trips/{id}/enroll` with your JWT. On success, the button is replaced by an **Inscrito** confirmation badge and your name is added to the participant list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Enrollment will fail if the trip has reached its maximum participant capacity (`maxim_participants`) or if you are already enrolled.
</Warning>

## Leaving a trip

If you need to leave a trip you have already joined:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the trip detail page">
    Navigate to `/trips/:id` for the trip you want to leave.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the leave action">
    The frontend sends `POST /viatge/trips/{id}/leave` with your JWT. Your enrollment record is removed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  After leaving a trip, the **Chat del Grupo** tab is no longer visible to you. You can re-enroll later if the trip still has capacity.
</Note>

## Using trip chat

The group chat is available on the **Chat del Grupo** tab of any trip you are enrolled in.

- The `TripChat` component loads messages for the trip and lets you send new ones in real time.
- The chat tab only appears when you are enrolled (`isEnrolled === true`) or when your role is `Admin`.
- To access the chat, click the **Chat del Grupo** tab on the trip detail page at `/trips/:id`.

<Tip>
  If you do not see the **Chat del Grupo** tab, you are not enrolled in that trip. Enroll first using the **Inscribirse ahora** button.
</Tip>

## Viewing and editing your profile

Your profile is at `/profile`. It calls `GET /users/users/me` to load your account information.

**Profile information displayed:**

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Email** | Your registered email address (read-only) |
| **Biografía** (`bio`) | A short personal bio, editable |
| **Rol Actual** | Your current role badge (Viajero / Creador / Administrador) |

**To edit your profile:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Editar Perfil">
    The button appears in the top-right of the account information section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your details">
    You can change your **Nombre Completo** (`full_name`) and **Biografía** (`bio`). Your email and role cannot be changed from this page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Guardar Cambios**. The frontend sends `PUT /users/users/me/{full_name}/{bio}` with your updated values.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Requesting a Creator promotion

If you want to create your own trips, you can request a promotion to the **Creador** role. This feature is only available to users with the `Viatger` role.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to /promotion">
    Go to `/promotion`. This route is protected — it is only accessible when you are logged in with the `Viatger` role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your justification">
    Fill in the **Justificación / Motivación** (`missatge`) field. Explain why you want to become a Creator and what types of travel experiences you would like to offer. This message is sent to the admin for review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the request">
    Click **Enviar Solicitud**. The frontend sends `POST /viatge/promote-request` with the body `{ "missatge": "your message" }`. On success, you see a confirmation and are redirected to `/dashboard` after a few seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for admin review">
    An admin will review your request from the `/admin/requests` panel and either approve or reject it. If approved, the admin must also change your role to `Creador`. Your next login will reflect the updated role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The `/promotion` route is removed from navigation once your role changes to `Creador` or `Admin`. You can only submit one promotion request at a time.
</Note>
